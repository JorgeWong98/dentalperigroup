Dr. Espinoza is an active member and participates in their continuing education programs and vigorously adheres to their strict code of ethics and standards in the following professional organizations:

Graduated from the University of Ciudad Juarez with his degree in General Dentistry in Ciudad Juarez, Mexico. Completed graduate studies at the University of Ciudad Juarez in Periodontics Ciudad Juarez, Mexico. Completed Masters Degree in Oral Implantology at the Biomedical Sciences Institute in Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, Mexico. Currently the doctor has completed the 1st of 3 phases of the National Board Dental Exam (NBDE) at the University of San Diego, California to become qualified as a dentist in the United States.

As a young man, he decided to help people to get healthy through oral health. As a Doctor worried about his patients, Dr. Espinoza dedicate part of his time to study the relationship with Periodontics disease and others systemic diseases like for example Diabetes Mellitus, Heart Disease, Premature Childbirths, and others. These studies have helped him understand relationships and better serve his patients total health care. Please click on this link to find out more about this important relationship: The Mouth-Body Connections.

The PERIOGROUP practice was established in 2004. It has a highly trained staff of technicians and assistants that makes your dental experience very pleasant. The staff and the doctor all speak both English and Spanish, which of course, make it much easier to understand your treatment options.
